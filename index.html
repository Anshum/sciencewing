About Us

Team of IISER Alumni/Scholars

Science Wing is an initiative by Ph.D scholars and alumni of IISER Bhopal to impart quality online classes to the CSIR-NET aspirants.

We have designed course for college goers, working professionals and other such candidates who are not able to attend regular classroom courses.

We are committed to deliver education of high standards at nominal rates through digital platform.
